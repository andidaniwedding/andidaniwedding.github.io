Getting There

To reach the venue, you can either take a taxi (recommended) or use public transport.

By Taxi
Taxis are the easiest and most convenient option for both arriving at and leaving the venue.

By Public Transport
You can take bus number 795 from Széll Kálmán tér to Budakeszi, Szőlőtelep. The bus runs every 30 minutes, but the last bus departs at 11 PM, so you’ll need a taxi for your return journey after that.
