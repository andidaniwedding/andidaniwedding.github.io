Getting There

By Car

The venue offers plenty of parking spaces. If you plan to drink alcohol, we recommend using this app to call a driver who can take you and your car home: https://soforhivo.hu/

By Taxi
Taxis are the easiest and most convenient option for both arriving at and leaving the venue.

By Public Transport

During the day, you can take bus number 795 from Széll Kálmán tér to Budakeszi, Szőlőtelep. The bus runs every 30 minutes, but the last bus departs at 11 PM

While there is no direct bus at night, the venue provides a free shuttle service to the night bus stop, where bus 922 depart for Széll Kálmán tér every 30 minutes.
